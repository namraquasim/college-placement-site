ECE UG Placement Statistics

The academic excellence of Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) is reflected in its student's exemplary record in placements in the corporate and engineering sectors. The Department has consistently maintained an excellent recruitment record. ECE graduates are recruited by some of the world's leading corporates like Texas Instruments India Pvt Ltd, Qualcomm India Pvt Ltd, SanDisk India Device Design Centre (P) ltd, etc., besides leading Indian companies such as Bharati Airtel Ltd., WIPRO, etc. Students are given training in soft skills, personality development and how to face interviews.
